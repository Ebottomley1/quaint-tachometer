Ed's Wieners:

Welcome to Ed's Wieners! We've been putting wieners in your buns since 2017! Click the Products tab to browse items available to purchase. These dogs are hand-made by Ed himself using the freshest ingredients on the market! To learn more about our hot-doggery, click the About tab. Click the Contact tab and give us a call to place an order which will be ready to pick up from our Salisbury location in just 10 minutes, or send us an email with some feedback; Ed's Wieners is constantly trying to grow!

Ed's Weiners was founded by Edward Bottomley in Salisbury, Maryland in February of 2017. The idea spawned when his Info 301 teacher made him complete a project, in which he had to create a fake website. He decided he wanted to make a company focused around one of his true loves: food! 'But what kind of food?' he asked himself. Hotdogs! Ed decided that hotdogs were easy enough for him to make on his own in large quantities and it allowed him to make a funny, crude-looking name for his company. Today, you can enjoy five different wieners, all handmade by Ed himself! So go ahead, put an Ed's Wiener in your mouth!
